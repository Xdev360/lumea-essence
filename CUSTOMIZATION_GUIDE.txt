<!-- ========================================
    COLOR CUSTOMIZATION REFERENCE
    ======================================== -->

Current Color Palette:
- Primary Gradient: #D4A5D4 → #F8C4E6
- Deep Purple: #8B5A8B
- Text: #4a4a4a (dark gray)
- Light Text: #6b6b6b (medium gray)
- Subtle Text: #a0a0a0 (light gray)
- Background: Gradient blend (pastel)

To change all colors:
1. Open styles.css
2. Use Find & Replace (Ctrl+H or Cmd+H)
3. Replace old hex with new hex:
   - #D4A5D4 → #YOUR_PRIMARY
   - #F8C4E6 → #YOUR_SECONDARY
   - #8B5A8B → #YOUR_ACCENT
